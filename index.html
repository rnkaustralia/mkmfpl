  • Our Story

    Mr. Nishit M Mehta a Pharmacist with expertise in manufacturing and sales of Pharmaceutical, Cosmetic and Chemical Products and Mr. Sanjay M Mehta (Chartered Accountant and financial expert) came together with the intentions consolidating various small firms and converting them into a proper & organised company hence M/s MKM Formulations Pvt Ltd was established in 2017.

    It went ahead and took over Firms like M/s Cosmo Pharma Industries, M/s Abheer Industries, M/s Rich Industries and formed a single company. Certain Directors / Owners were retired, Redundant machinery was discarded and a proper setup was created. Currently M/s MKMFPL has systematic manufacturing facilities with demarcated areas for Production, packaging and storage of Raw Material, Packing Materials and finished goods. Proper Quality Control facilities were also established to ensure consumers get a good and consistent product. Research and Development activities were later introduced for development of existing product range and create more and more products in line with the changing consumer needs. We further found that consumers do not have good clean eco-friendly options for their uses hence we are now working towards making our existing products more eco friendly and create new product substitutes that are eco-friendly.

    Our mission at MKMFPL is to address every consumer cleaning and perfuming need with easy to use, safe to handle, High Quality and eco friendly products for their day to day use. We work with you in regenerating your ambience. It is our endeavour to create a Clean Aromatic & Beautiful Ambience around every individual.
